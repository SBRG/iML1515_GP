CC   -!- FUNCTION: Na(+)/H(+) antiporter that extrudes sodium in exchange
CC       for external protons. Catalyzes the exchange of 2 H(+) per Na(+).
CC       Can mediate sodium uptake when a transmembrane pH gradient is
CC       applied. Active at alkaline pH. Activity is strongly down-
CC       regulated below pH 6.5. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1645730}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Monomer and homodimer. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11258962,

CC       protein.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Transcription stimulated by high Na(+) concentrations.
