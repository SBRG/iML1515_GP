CC       fully oxidized in vivo.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Mutations alter the permeability of the
CC       outer membrane resulting in increased sensitivity to detergents,
CC       antibiotics and dyes. Depletion leads to filamentation followed by
CC       membrane rupture and cell lysis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12207697,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2547691}.
