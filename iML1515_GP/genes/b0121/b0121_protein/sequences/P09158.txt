CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the biosynthesis of polyamines which play a
CC       significant role in the structural and functional organization in
CC       the chromoid of E.coli by compacting DNA and neutralizing negative
CC       charges. Catalyzes the irreversible transfer (ping-pong mechanism)
CC       of a propylamine group from the amino donor S-
CC       adenosylmethioninamine (decarboxy-AdoMet) to putrescine (1,4-
CC       diaminobutane) to yield spermidine. Cadaverine (1,5-
CC       diaminopentane) and spermidine can also be used as the propylamine
CC       acceptor. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23001854,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl 3-(methylthio)propylamine +
CC       putrescine = 5'-S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine + spermidine.
CC       {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00198, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23001854,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl 3-(methylthio)propylamine +
CC       spermidine = S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine + spermine.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl 3-(methylthio)propylamine + 1,5-
CC       diaminopentane = S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine + N-(3-aminopropyl)-
CC       1,5-diaminopentane. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl 3-(methylthio)propylamine +
CC       spermidine = S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine + thermospermine + H(+).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: The activity is thought to be regulated mainly
CC       by the availability of decarboxylated S-adenosylmethionine.
CC       Inhibited by dicyclohexylamine, S-adenosyl-1,8-diamino-3-
CC       thiooctane (AdoDATO), sulfonium-deaminated analogs of S-
CC       adenosyl(5')-3-methylthiopropylamine, p-hydroxymercuribenzoate and
CC       N-ethylmaleimide. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:4572733,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:6406265, ECO:0000269|PubMed:6508744,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:6993485}.
