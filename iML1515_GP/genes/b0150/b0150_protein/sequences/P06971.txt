CC   -!- FUNCTION: This receptor binds the ferrichrome-iron ligand. It
CC       interacts with the TonB protein, which is responsible for energy
CC       coupling of the ferrichrome-promoted iron transport system. Acts
CC       as a receptor for bacteriophage T5 as well as T1, phi80 and
CC       colicin M. Binding of T5 triggers the opening of a high
CC       conductance ion channel. Can also transport the antibiotic
CC       albomycin. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8617231}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Monomer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell outer membrane; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Induced 1.6-fold by hydroxyurea.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20005847}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the TonB-dependent receptor family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
