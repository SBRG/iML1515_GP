CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the ABC transporter complex FhuCDB involved in
CC       iron(3+)-hydroxamate import. Binds the iron(3+)-hydroxamate
CC       complex and transfers it to the membrane-bound permease. Required
CC       for the transport of all iron(3+)-hydroxamate siderophores such as
CC       ferrichrome, gallichrome, desferrioxamine, coprogen, aerobactin,
CC       shizokinen, rhodotorulic acid and the antibiotic albomycin.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10742172, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11805094,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2254301, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8522527}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex is composed of two ATP-binding proteins
CC       (FhuC), two transmembrane proteins (FhuB) and a solute-binding
CC       protein (FhuD) (Probable). FhuD interacts with FhuB
CC       (PubMed:8522527, PubMed:9426146). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8522527,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9426146, ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17218314}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Induced 1.3-fold by hydroxyurea.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20005847}.
CC   -!- PTM: Exported by the Tat system. The position of the signal
CC       peptide cleavage has been experimentally proven. Can also be
CC       exported by the Sec system. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17218314}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the bacterial solute-binding protein 8
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
