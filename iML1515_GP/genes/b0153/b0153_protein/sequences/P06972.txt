CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the ABC transporter complex FhuCDB involved in

CC       the substrate across the membrane. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1551849,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:3020380}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex is composed of two ATP-binding proteins
CC       (FhuC), two transmembrane proteins (FhuB) and a solute-binding
CC       protein (FhuD) (Probable). FhuB interacts with FhuC and FhuD
CC       (PubMed:1551849, PubMed:8522527, PubMed:9426146).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1551849, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8522527,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9426146, ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Induced 2.1-fold by hydroxyurea.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20005847}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: FhuD interacts with a periplasmic and a
CC       transmembrane/cytoplasmic region of FhuB. The transmembrane region
CC       may be part of a pore through which a portion of FhuD inserts into
CC       the cytoplasmic membrane during transport.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9426146}.
