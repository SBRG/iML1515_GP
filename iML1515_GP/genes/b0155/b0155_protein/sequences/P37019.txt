CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proton-coupled chloride transporter. Functions as
CC       antiport system and exchanges two chloride ions for 1 proton.
CC       Probably acts as an electrical shunt for an outwardly-directed
CC       proton pump that is linked to amino acid decarboxylation, as part
CC       of the extreme acid resistance (XAR) response.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12384697, ECO:0000269|PubMed:14985752,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16341087, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16905147,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18678918}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18678918}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-
CC       pass membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: By acid-shock conditions.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the chloride channel (TC 2.A.49) family.
CC       ClcA subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- CAUTION: Was originally thought to be a voltage-gated ClC-type
CC       chloride channel. {ECO:0000305}.
