CC   -!- FUNCTION: Dipeptidase with broad substrate specificity. Requires
CC       dipeptide substrates with an unblocked N-terminus and the amino
CC       group in the alpha or beta position. Non-protein amino acids and
CC       proline are not accepted in the C-terminal position, whereas some
CC       dipeptide amides and formyl amino acids are hydrolyzed. Also shows
CC       cysteinylglycinase activity, which is sufficient for E.coli to
CC       utilize cysteinylglycine as a cysteine source.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11157967, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20067529,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:355237, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7988883}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Hydrolysis of dipeptides, preferentially
CC       hydrophobic dipeptides including prolyl amino acids.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:355237, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7988883}.
