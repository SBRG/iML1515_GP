CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the catabolism of short chain fatty acids
CC       (SCFA) via the tricarboxylic acid (TCA)(acetyl degradation route)
CC       and via the 2-methylcitrate cycle I (propionate degradation
CC       route). Catalyzes the Claisen condensation of propionyl-CoA and
CC       oxaloacetate (OAA) to yield (2S,3S)-2-methylcitrate (2-MC) and
CC       CoA. Also catalyzes the condensation of oxaloacetate with acetyl-
CC       CoA to yield citrate but with a lower specificity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8508809, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9325432,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9579066}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Propanoyl-CoA + H(2)O + oxaloacetate =
CC       (2S,3S)-2-hydroxybutane-1,2,3-tricarboxylate + CoA.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9325432, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9579066}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Acetyl-CoA + H(2)O + oxaloacetate = citrate +
CC       CoA. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9325432, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9579066}.

CC         {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9325432};

CC         {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9579066};
