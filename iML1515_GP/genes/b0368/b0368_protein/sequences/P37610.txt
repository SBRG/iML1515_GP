CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the conversion of taurine and alpha
CC       ketoglutarate to sulfite, aminoacetaldehyde and succinate.
CC       Required for the utilization of taurine (2-aminoethanesulfonic
CC       acid) as an alternative sulfur source. Pentane-sulfonic acid, 3-
CC       (N-morpholino)propanesulfonic acid and 1,3-dioxo-2-
CC       isoindolineethanesulfonic acid are also substrates for this
CC       enzyme.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Taurine + 2-oxoglutarate + O(2) = sulfite +
