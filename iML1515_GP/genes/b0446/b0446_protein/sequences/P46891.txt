CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the hydrolysis of 4-amino-2-methyl-5-
CC       hydroxymethylpyrimidine pyrophosphate (HMP-PP) to 4-amino-2-
CC       methyl-5-hydroxymethylpyrimidine phosphate (HMP-P). Can also
CC       hydrolyze other substrates such as MeO-HMP-PP and 4-amino-2-
CC       trifluoromethyl 5-hydroxymethylpyrimidine pyrophosphate (CF3-HMP-
CC       PP) to give MeO-HMP-P and 4-amino-2-trifluoromethyl-5-
CC       hydroxymethylpyrimidine phosphate. This hydrolysis generates
CC       resistance to the antibiotics (bacimethrin, CF3-HMP) by reducing
CC       the formation of their toxic forms, 2'-methoxythiamin
CC       pyrophosphate (MeO-TPP) and CF3-HMP-PP. Also hydrolyzes pyridoxal-
CC       phosphate (PLP) and flavin mononucleotide (FMN), and purines (GMP
CC       and IMP) as secondary substrates. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-
