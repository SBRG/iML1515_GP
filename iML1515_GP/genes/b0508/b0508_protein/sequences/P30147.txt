CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reversible isomerization between
CC       hydroxypyruvate and 2-hydroxy-3-oxopropanoate (also termed
CC       tartronate semialdehyde). Does not catalyze the isomerization of
CC       D-fructose to D-glucose or that of D-xylulose to D-xylose. Also
CC       does not catalyze racemization of serine, alanine, glycerate or
CC       lactate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10561547}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Hydroxypyruvate = 2-hydroxy-3-oxopropanoate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10561547}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Not stimulated by addition of pyridoxal 5'-
CC       phosphate (0.1 mM), FAD, NAD(+), NADP(+) or ATP (1 mM each). EDTA
CC       (10 mM) and metal ions (1 mM) such as Ca(2+), Co(2+), Mg(2+),
CC       Ni(2+), Zn(2+) do not affect the enzyme activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10561547}.
