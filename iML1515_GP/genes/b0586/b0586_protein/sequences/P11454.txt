CC       three amide and three ester bonds present in the cyclic (2,3-
CC       dihydroxybenzoyl)serine trimer enterobactin, using seryladenylate
CC       and acyl-holo-EntB (acylated with 2,3-dihydroxybenzoate by EntE).
