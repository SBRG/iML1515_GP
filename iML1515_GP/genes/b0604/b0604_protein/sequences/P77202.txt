CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in disulfide bond formation. DsbG and DsbC are
CC       part of a periplasmic reducing system that controls the level of
CC       cysteine sulfenylation, and provides reducing equivalents to
CC       rescue oxidatively damaged secreted proteins such as ErfK, YbiS
CC       and YnhG. Probably also functions as a disulfide isomerase with a
CC       narrower substrate specificity than DsbC. DsbG is maintained in a
CC       reduced state by DsbD. Displays chaperone activity in both redox
CC       states in vitro. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19965429}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with ErfK, YbiS and YnhG.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17372350, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19965429}.
