CC       morphology defects of the topoisomerase IV temperature-sensitive
CC       mutants (PubMed:12904550). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12904550,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22194412}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Overexpression of pagP (crcA), cspE and crcB leads
CC       to camphor resistance, chromosome condensation and suppression of
CC       growth defects of mukB mutants (PubMed:8844142). Overexpression
CC       also increases levels of plasmid supercoiling, results in
CC       increased resistance to nalidixic acid in gyrase and topoisomerase
CC       IV temperature-sensitive mutants and affects the regulation of
CC       several genes (PubMed:12904550). {ECO:0000305|PubMed:12904550,
CC       ECO:0000305|PubMed:8844142}.
