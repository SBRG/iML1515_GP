CC   -!- FUNCTION: A terminal oxidase that produces a proton motive force
CC       by the vectorial transfer of protons across the inner membrane. It
CC       is the component of the aerobic respiratory chain of E.coli that
CC       predominates when cells are grown at low aeration. Generates a
CC       proton motive force using protons and electrons from opposite
CC       sides of the membrane to generate H(2)O, transferring 1
CC       proton/electron. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19542282,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21987791, ECO:0000269|PubMed:6307994}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 2 ubiquinol + O(2) + 4 H(+)(Side 1) = 2
CC       ubiquinone + 2 H(2)O + 4 H(+)(Side 2).
