CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in zinc efflux across the cytoplasmic membrane,
CC       thus reducing zinc accumulation in the cytoplasm and rendering
CC       bacteria more resistant to zinc. It may contribute to zinc
CC       homeostasis at low concentrations of zinc, whereas ZntA is
CC       required for growth at more toxic concentrations.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: By zinc.
