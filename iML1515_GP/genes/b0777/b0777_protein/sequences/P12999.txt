CC   -!- FUNCTION: Converts the free carboxyl group of a malonyl-thioester
CC       to its methyl ester by transfer of a methyl group from S-adenosyl-
CC       L-methionine (SAM). It allows to synthesize pimeloyl-ACP via the
CC       fatty acid synthetic pathway. E.coli employs a methylation and
CC       demethylation strategy to allow elongation of a temporarily
CC       disguised malonate moiety to a pimelate moiety by the fatty acid
CC       synthetic enzymes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20693992,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:4864413}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl-L-methionine + malonyl-[acyl-

CC       carrier protein] methyl ester. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20693992}.
