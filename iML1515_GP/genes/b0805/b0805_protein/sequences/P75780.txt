CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the active transport across the outer
CC       membrane of iron complexed with catecholate siderophores such as
CC       dihydroxybenzoylserine and dihydroxybenzoate. It derives its
CC       energy for transport by interacting with the trans-periplasmic
CC       membrane protein TonB. Can also transport catechol-substituted
CC       cephalosporins. Receptor for microcins M, H47 and E492.
