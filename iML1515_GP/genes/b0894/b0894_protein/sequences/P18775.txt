CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reduction of dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO) to
CC       dimethyl sulfide (DMS). DMSO reductase serves as the terminal
CC       reductase under anaerobic conditions, with DMSO being the terminal
CC       electron acceptor. Terminal reductase during anaerobic growth on
CC       various sulfoxides and N-oxide compounds. Allows E.coli to grow
CC       anaerobically on DMSO as respiratory oxidant.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:3062312}.
