CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Several mechanisms have been proposed based on
CC       complexes formed with substrate analogs. After activation by the

CC       hydrogen atoms from the other active site cysteine, Cys-419, and
CC       from coenzyme A, and it can also transfer hydrogen atoms to
CC       product radicals. The other active site cysteine can attack the
