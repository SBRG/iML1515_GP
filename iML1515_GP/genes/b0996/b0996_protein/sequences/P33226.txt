CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the anaerobic respiratory chain of
CC       trimethylamine-N-oxide reductase TorA. Acts by transferring
CC       electrons from the membranous menaquinones to TorA. This transfer
CC       probably involves an electron transfer pathway from menaquinones
CC       to the N-terminal domain of TorC, then from the N-terminus to the
CC       C-terminus, and finally to TorA. TorC apocytochrome negatively
CC       autoregulates the torCAD operon probably by inhibiting the TorS
CC       kinase activity.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The N-terminal domain interacts with TorA. The immature
CC       C-terminal domain can bind to the N-terminal detector region of
CC       TorS.

CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane; Single-pass type II
CC       membrane protein.
