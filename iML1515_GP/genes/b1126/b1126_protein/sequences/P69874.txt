CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8366082}.

CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Strongly inhibited by carbonyl cyanide m-

CC       interaction with the C-terminus. Also inhibited by N-

CC       inhibited by KCN. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11976340,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2249996, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7592703,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8366082}.
