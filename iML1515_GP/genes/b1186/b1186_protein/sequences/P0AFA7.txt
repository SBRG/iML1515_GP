CC   -!- FUNCTION: Na(+)/H(+) antiporter that extrudes sodium in exchange
CC       for external protons. Catalyzes the exchange of 3 H(+) per 2
CC       Na(+). Has a high affinity for sodium, but can also transport
CC       lithium. Activity is weakly pH-dependent. Essential for regulation
CC       of intracellular pH under alkaline conditions.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:7822245, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7929345,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8019504, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8093613}.
