CC   -!- FUNCTION: Chaperone required for proper molybdenum cofactor
CC       insertion and final assembly of the membrane-bound respiratory
CC       nitrate reductase 1. Required for the insertion of the molybdenum
CC       into the apo-NarG subunit, maybe by keeping NarG in an appropriate
CC       competent-open conformation for the molybdenum cofactor insertion
CC       to occur. NarJ maintains the apoNarGH complex in a soluble state.
CC       Upon insertion of the molybdenum cofactor, NarJ seems to
CC       dissociate from the activated soluble NarGH complex, before its
CC       association with the NarI subunit on the membrane.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16286471, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1732220,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9305880, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9632249}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds specifically to the NarG subunit of the apoenzyme
CC       complex at two distinct sites, one interfering with membrane
CC       anchoring and another being involved in molybdenum insertion.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16286471, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9305880,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9632249}.
