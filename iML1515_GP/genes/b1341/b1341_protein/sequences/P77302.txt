CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of a 2 protein system that seems to be dedicated to
CC       regulate transcription of csgD. The CsdG protein in turn regulates
CC       expression of adhesive curli fimbriae genes csgEFG, csgBAC/ymaD
CC       and adrA (yaic). Activity of this protein is antagonized by the
CC       phosphodiesterase Gmr (YciR). In vitro has weak diguanylate
CC       cyclase activity. Cyclic-di-GMP is a second messenger which
CC       controls cell surface-associated traits in bacteria.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17010156}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 2 GTP = 2 diphosphate + cyclic di-3',5'-
CC       guanylate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17010156}.

CC   -!- INDUCTION: Expressed during transition into stationary phase,
CC       expression is higher at 28 than 37 degrees Celsius, more highly
CC       expressed on plates than in liquid medium. In rich medium YdaM and

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17010156, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19332833}.
