CC   -!- FUNCTION: Low-affinity potassium transport system. Interacts with
CC       Trk system potassium uptake protein TrkA. Requires TrkE (sapD) for
CC       maximal transport activity, low activity is seen in its absence;
CC       no further stimulation is seen with SapF (PubMed:11700350).
CC       Transport in the absence of SapD is dependent on a high membrane
CC       potential and a high cytoplasmic ATP concentration, suggesting
CC       this protein may be able to interact with other ATP-binding
CC       proteins (PubMed:11700350). Can transport potassium and rubidium
CC       (PubMed:7896723). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11700350,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2022616, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2674131,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7896723}.
