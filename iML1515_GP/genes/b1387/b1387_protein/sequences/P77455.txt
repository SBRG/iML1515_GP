CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the hydrolytic ring cleavage of 2-oxepin-
CC       2(3H)-ylideneacetyl-CoA (oxepin-CoA) via the open-chain aldehyde
CC       intermediate to yield 3-oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA. The enzyme
CC       consists of a C-terminal (R)-specific enoyl-CoA hydratase domain
CC       (formerly MaoC) that cleaves the ring and produces the highly
CC       reactive 3-oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA semialdehyde and an N-
CC       terminal NADP-dependent aldehyde dehydrogenase domain that
CC       oxidizes the aldehyde to 3-oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA. Can also
CC       use crotonyl-CoA as substrate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20660314,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21296885, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9748275}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 2-oxepin-2(3H)-ylideneacetyl-CoA + H(2)O = 3-
CC       oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA semialdehyde.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21296885}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 3-oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA semialdehyde +
CC       NADP(+) + H(2)O = 3-oxo-5,6-dehydrosuberyl-CoA + NADPH.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21296885}.
