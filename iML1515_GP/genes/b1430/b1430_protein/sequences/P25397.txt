CC   -!- FUNCTION: S-adenosyl-L-methionine dependent methyltransferase that
CC       catalyzes the methylation of tellurite and is responsible for
CC       tellurite resistance when present in high copy number. Can also
CC       methylate selenite and selenium dioxide. Is thus able to detoxify
CC       different chalcogens. Cannot methylate arsenic compounds.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11053398, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21244361}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl-L-methionine + tellurite = S-
CC       adenosyl-L-homocysteine + methanetelluronate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21244361}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:11053398}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000305|PubMed:11053398}.
CC       Note=Probably a peripheral membrane protein that interacts with
CC       TehA.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the TehB family. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- CAUTION: Was originally thought to be plasmid encoded.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:2060788}.
