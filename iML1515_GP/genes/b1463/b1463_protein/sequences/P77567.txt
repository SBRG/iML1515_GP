CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the acetyl-CoA-dependent N-acetylation of
CC       aromatic amines, and, probably, the O-acetylation of N-
CC       hydroxyarylamines. In vitro, catalyzes the N-acetylation of
CC       various arylamines such as aminobenzoic acid, aminophenol,
CC       aminotoluene, phenetidine, anisidine, aniline, isoniazid and 2-
CC       amino-fluorene. N-hydroxyarylamine O-acetyltransferase activity
CC       has not been assayed directly, however, NhoA activity is required
CC       for the mutagenicity of nitroaromatic compounds, suggesting that
CC       it also has O-acetyltransferase activity.
