CC   -!- FUNCTION: A diguanylate cyclase, overexpression leads to a strong
CC       repression of swimming; swimming returnes to normal when residues
CC       206-207 are both mutated to Ala. Overexpression also leads to a
CC       reduction in flagellar abundance and a 20-fold increase in c-di-
CC       GMP levels in vivo. Required for aminoglycoside-mediated induction
CC       of biofilm formation, it also plays a lesser role in biofilm
CC       production in response to other classes of translation inhibitors.
CC       The c-di-GMP produced by this enzyme up-regulates poly-GlcNAc
CC       production as well as the biofilm synthesis protein PgaD, although
CC       c-di-GMP is probably not the main inducing principle. Cyclic-di-
CC       GMP is a second messenger which controls cell surface-associated
CC       traits in bacteria. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19460094}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 2 GTP = 2 diphosphate + cyclic di-3',5'-
CC       guanylate.
