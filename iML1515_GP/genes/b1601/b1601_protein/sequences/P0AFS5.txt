CC   -!- FUNCTION: Controls the transport of the quorum-sensing signal AI-2
CC       either by enhancing its secretion or inhibiting its uptake and
CC       consequently represses biofilm formation and motility and affects
CC       the global gene expression in biofilms.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385049}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells have a decreased extracellular and
CC       increased intracellular concentration of AI-2. Deletion of tqsA
CC       results in a 7000-fold increase in biofilm thickness and 574-fold
CC       increase in biomass in flow cells. Deletion of tqsA increases cell
CC       motility by increasing transcription of flagellar genes. The tqsA
CC       deletion mutant shows higher resistance toward crystal violet,
CC       spectinomycin, streptomycin sulfate, 2,6-dichloroquinone-4-
CC       chloroimide, chloramphenicol and amoxicillin.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385049}.
