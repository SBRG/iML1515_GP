CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reversible hydration of fumarate to (S)-
CC       malate. Functions as an aerobic enzyme in the direction of malate
CC       formation as part of the citric acid cycle. Accounts for about 80%
CC       of the fumarase activity when the bacteria grow aerobically. To a
CC       lesser extent, also displays D-tartrate dehydratase activity in
CC       vitro, but is not able to convert (R)-malate, L-tartrate or meso-
CC       tartrate. Can also catalyze the isomerization of enol- to keto-
CC       oxaloacetate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1329945,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23405168, ECO:0000269|PubMed:3282546,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8422384, ECO:0000269|Ref.7}.
