CC       which leads to the biosynthesis of aromatic amino acids (AroAA).
CC       Catalyzes the cis-dehydration of 3-dehydroquinate (DHQ) and
CC       introduces the first double bond of the aromatic ring to yield 3-
CC       dehydroshikimate. The reaction involves the formation of an imine
CC       intermediate between the keto group of 3-dehydroquinate and the
CC       epsylon-amino group of a lys-170 at the active site.
