CC   -!- FUNCTION: CoA transferase having broad substrate specificity for
CC       short-chain acyl-CoA thioesters with the activity decreasing when
CC       the length of the carboxylic acid chain exceeds four carbons. May
CC       play a role in short-chain fatty acid metabolism in E.coli (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Acyl-CoA + acetate = a fatty acid anion +
CC       acetyl-CoA.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer; dimer of dimers. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Formation of the covalent enzyme-CoA thioester
CC       intermediate proceeds via an unstable anhydride species formed
CC       between the carboxylate group of the catalytic glutamate of the
CC       enzyme and the carbonyl carbon of the thioester linkage of the
CC       substrate. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the 3-oxoacid CoA-transferase family.
