CC       the cyclical salvage pathway for production of NAD from
CC       nicotinamide (PubMed:4399474). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:4399474,
CC       ECO:0000305|PubMed:8726014}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Is also able to hydrolyze the first-line antituberculous
CC       drug pyrazinamide (PZA) into pyrazinoic acid in vitro, but this
CC       reaction is not considered to be physiologically relevant.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:8726014}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Nicotinamide + H(2)O = nicotinate + NH(3).
