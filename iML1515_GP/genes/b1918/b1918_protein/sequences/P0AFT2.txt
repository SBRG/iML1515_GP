CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the ABC transporter complex FliY-YecC-YecS
CC       involved in L-cystine transport. The system can probably also
CC       transport L-cysteine, and it mediates accumulation of the toxic
CC       compounds L-selenaproline (SCA) and L-selenocystine (SeCys)
CC       (PubMed:25139244). Probably responsible for the translocation of
CC       the substrate across the membrane (Probable).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25139244, ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: The FliY-YecC-YecS system is inhibited by L-
CC       cystine, L-cysteine, DL-2,6-diaminopimelic acid and L-
CC       cystathionine, and is stimulated by D-cysteine.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25139244}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex is composed of two ATP-binding proteins
CC       (YecC), two transmembrane proteins (YecS) and a solute-binding
CC       protein (FliY). {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: The yecS-ydjN double mutant is completely
CC       resistant to both L-selenaproline and L-selenocystine.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25139244}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the binding-protein-dependent transport
CC       system permease family. HisMQ subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
