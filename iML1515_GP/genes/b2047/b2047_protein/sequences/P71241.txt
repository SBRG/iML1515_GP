CC   -!- FUNCTION: Is the initiating enzyme for colanic acid (CA)
CC       synthesis. Catalyzes the transfer of the glucose-1-phosphate
CC       moiety from UDP-Glc onto the carrier lipid undecaprenyl phosphate
CC       (C55-P), forming a phosphoanhydride bond yielding to glucosyl-
CC       pyrophosphoryl-undecaprenol (Glc-PP-C55). Also possesses a weak
CC       galactose-1-P transferase activity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: UDP-glucose + ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl
CC       phosphate = UMP + alpha-D-glucopyranosyl-diphospho-
CC       ditrans,octacis-undecaprenol. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Exopolysaccharide biosynthesis; colanic acid
CC       biosynthesis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells lacking this gene show a non-mucoid
CC       phenotype. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Complements holdfast synthesis in the C.crescentus
CC       mutant lacking hfsE, pssY and pssZ genes. Can also partially
CC       complement the O-antigen defect in the S.typhimurium wbaP deletion
CC       strain (PubMed:22408159). {ECO:0000305|PubMed:22408159}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the bacterial sugar transferase family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
