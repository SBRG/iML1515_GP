CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the tagatose-1,6-bisphosphate aldolase

CC       any aldolase activity. Is involved in the catabolism of
CC       galactitol. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11976750,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8955298}.
