CC       physiologically the reduction of uracil to 5,6-dihydrouracil (DHU)

CC       reverse reaction and the reduction of thymine to 5,6-
CC       dihydrothymine (DHT). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18482579,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21169495}.

CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 5,6-dihydrothymine + NAD(+) = thymine + NADH.
