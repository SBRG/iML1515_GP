CC   -!- FUNCTION: The phosphoenolpyruvate-dependent sugar
CC       phosphotransferase system (sugar PTS), a major carbohydrate active
CC       transport system, catalyzes the phosphorylation of incoming sugar
CC       substrates concomitantly with their translocation across the cell
CC       membrane. The enzyme II FruAB PTS system is involved in fructose
CC       transport. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:3510127,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8626640, ECO:0000305|PubMed:3076173}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: [Protein]-N(pi)-phospho-L-histidine + D-
CC       fructose(Side 1) = [protein]-L-histidine + D-fructose 1-
CC       phosphate(Side 2). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8626640,
CC       ECO:0000305|PubMed:3510127}.

CC   -!- DOMAIN: The PTS EIIB type-2 domain is phosphorylated by phospho-
CC       EIIA on a cysteinyl residue. Then, it transfers the phosphoryl
CC       group to the sugar substrate concomitantly with the sugar uptake
CC       processed by the PTS EIIC type-2 domain. {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-
CC       ProRule:PRU00422}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: In the N-terminal, the PTS system fructose-specific
CC       possesses a duplicated EIIB domain (EIIB' domain) which lacks the
CC       active site and functions to facilitate phosphoryl transfer
CC       between the EIIA domain of diphosphoryl transfer protein (DTP) and
CC       the EIIB domain. Construction of a protein lacking the EIIB'
CC       domain shows that it is functional for fructose transport in vivo
CC       as well as fructose phosphorylation in vitro. The presence of the
CC       EIIB' domain, however, is required for normal high affinity
CC       recognition of DTP by the PTS system fructose-specific as well as
CC       for normal rates of phosphoryl transfer between the EIIA and EIIB
CC       domains of DTP and PTS system fructose-specific, respectively.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8626640}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The EIIC type-2 domain forms the PTS system translocation
CC       channel and contains the specific substrate-binding site.
CC       {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00427}.
