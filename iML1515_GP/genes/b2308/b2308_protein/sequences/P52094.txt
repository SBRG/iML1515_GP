CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the histidine permease ABC transporter. Also
CC       part of a lysine/arginine/ornithine transporter. Probably
CC       responsible for the translocation of the substrate across the
CC       membrane. Required to relay the ATPase-inducing signal from the
CC       solute-binding protein to HisP (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex is composed of two ATP-binding proteins
CC       (HisP), two transmembrane proteins (HisM and HisQ) and a solute-
CC       binding protein (HisJ or ArgT). {ECO:0000250}.
