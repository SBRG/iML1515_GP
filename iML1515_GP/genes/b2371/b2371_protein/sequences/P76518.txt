CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the catabolism of oxalate and in the
CC       adapatation to low pH. ACOCT serves to prime the oxalate-induced
CC       acid tolerance response (ATR) cycle by producing substrate for
CC       oxalyl-CoA decarboxylase (OXC) and formyl-coenzyme A transferase
CC       (FCOCT). Catalyzes the reversible conversion of acetyl-CoA and
CC       oxalate to oxalyl-CoA and acetate. It can also use formyl-CoA and
CC       oxalate to produce oxalyl-CoA and formate with significantly
CC       reduced specific activity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23935849}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Acetyl-CoA + oxalate = acetate + oxalyl-CoA.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23935849}.
