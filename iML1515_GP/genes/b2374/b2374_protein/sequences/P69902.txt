CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the catabolism of oxalate and in the
CC       adapatation to low pH via the induction of the oxalate-dependent
CC       acid tolerance response (ATR). Catalyzes the transfer of the CoA
CC       moiety from formyl-CoA to oxalate. It can also use succinate as an
CC       acceptor. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12844490,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18245280, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23335415}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Formyl-CoA + oxalate = formate + oxalyl-CoA.
CC       {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00742, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18245280}.

CC         30 degrees Celsius) {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18245280};
CC         KM=510 uM for oxalate (with formyl-CoA as donor at pH 6.7 and 30
