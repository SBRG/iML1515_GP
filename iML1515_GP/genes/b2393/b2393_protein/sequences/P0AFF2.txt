CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transports nucleosides with a high affinity except
CC       guanosine and deoxyguanosine. Driven by a proton motive force.
CC       Transports cytidine, uridine, thymidine, adenosine and inosine.
CC       Can also transport xanthosine, but with a very low affinity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11466294, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15678184,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:374403}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Binding of nucleosides to NupC requires the
CC       presence of hydroxyl groups at the C-3' position of ribose.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15678184}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the concentrative nucleoside transporter
CC       (CNT) (TC 2.A.41) family. {ECO:0000305}.
