CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reversible transfer of the terminal
CC       phosphate of ATP to form a long-chain polyphosphate (polyP). Can
CC       form linear polymers of orthophosphate with chain lengths up to
CC       1000 or more. Can use GTP instead of ATP, but the efficiency of
CC       GTP is 5% that of ATP. Also exhibits several other enzymatic
CC       activities, which include: ATP synthesis from polyP in the
CC       presence of excess ADP, general nucleoside-diphosphate kinase
CC       activity, linear guanosine 5'-tetraphosphate (ppppG) synthesis and
CC       autophosphorylation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10660553,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8962061}.
