CC   -!- FUNCTION: Truncated proteins consisting of the GGDEF/EAL domains
CC       (residues 319-747) or of the EAL domain alone (481-747) have c-di-
CC       GMP phosphodiesterase activity. They do not have diguanylate
CC       cyclase activity. Cyclic-di-GMP is a second messenger which
CC       controls cell surface-associated traits in bacteria.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20522491}.
