CC   -!- FUNCTION: Master enzyme that delivers sulfur to a number of
CC       partners involved in Fe-S cluster assembly, tRNA modification or
CC       cofactor biosynthesis. Catalyzes the removal of elemental sulfur
CC       from cysteine to produce alanine. Functions as a sulfur delivery
CC       protein for Fe-S cluster synthesis onto IscU, an Fe-S scaffold
CC       assembly protein, as well as other S acceptor proteins.
CC       Preferentially binds to disordered IscU on which the Fe-S is
CC       assembled, IscU converts to the structured state and then
CC       dissociates from IscS to transfer the Fe-S to an acceptor protein.
CC       Also functions as a selenium delivery protein in the pathway for
CC       the biosynthesis of selenophosphate. Transfers sulfur onto 'Cys-
CC       456' of ThiI and onto 'Cys-19' of TusA in transpersulfidation
CC       reactions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10544286,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10600118, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10781558,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10781607, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10829016,
