CC   -!- FUNCTION: Stereospecific condensation of phosphoenolpyruvate (PEP)
CC       and D-erythrose-4-phosphate (E4P) giving rise to 3-deoxy-D-
CC       arabino-heptulosonate-7-phosphate (DAHP).
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Phosphoenolpyruvate + D-erythrose 4-phosphate
CC       + H(2)O = 3-deoxy-D-arabino-hept-2-ulosonate 7-phosphate +
CC       phosphate.
CC   -!- COFACTOR:
CC       Name=a divalent metal cation; Xref=ChEBI:CHEBI:60240;
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Specifically feedback inhibited by tyrosine
CC       with 50% inhibition observed at 9 microM tyrosine, pH 7.0.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Metabolic intermediate biosynthesis; chorismate
CC       biosynthesis; chorismate from D-erythrose 4-phosphate and
CC       phosphoenolpyruvate: step 1/7.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: There are 3 DAHP synthases, AroF is feedback-
CC       inhibited by Tyr. The other 2 DAHP synthases are Phe- and Trp-
CC       sensitive, respectively.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the class-I DAHP synthase family.
