CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reversible aldol-ketol isomerization
CC       between D-ribulose 5-phosphate (Ru5P) and D-arabinose 5-phosphate
CC       (A5P). It appears that the physiological function of G-API may be
CC       to synthesize the regulatory molecule A5P, which in turn
CC       participates in the induction of the gut operon through an unknown
CC       mechanism. It is also able of sustaining the biosynthetic pathway
CC       of 3-deoxy-D-manno-octulosonate (KDO), a unique 8-carbon sugar
CC       component of lipopolysaccharides (LPSs).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16199563}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: D-arabinose 5-phosphate = D-ribulose 5-
