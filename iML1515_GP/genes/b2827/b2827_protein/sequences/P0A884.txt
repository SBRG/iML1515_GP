CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the reductive methylation of 2'-deoxyuridine-
CC       5'-monophosphate (dUMP) to 2'-deoxythymidine-5'-monophosphate
CC       (dTMP) while utilizing 5,10-methylenetetrahydrofolate (mTHF) as
CC       the methyl donor and reductant in the reaction, yielding
CC       dihydrofolate (DHF) as a by-product (PubMed:3286637,
CC       PubMed:2223754, PubMed:9826509). This enzymatic reaction provides
CC       an intracellular de novo source of dTMP, an essential precursor
CC       for DNA biosynthesis. This protein also binds to its mRNA thus
CC       repressing its own translation (PubMed:7708505).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:2223754, ECO:0000269|PubMed:3286637,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7708505, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9826509}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 5,10-methylenetetrahydrofolate + dUMP =
