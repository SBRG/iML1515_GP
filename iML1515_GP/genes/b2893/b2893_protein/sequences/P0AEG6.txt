CC   -!- FUNCTION: Acts as a disulfide isomerase, interacting with
CC       incorrectly folded proteins to correct non-native disulfide bonds.
CC       DsbG and DsbC are part of a periplasmic reducing system that
CC       controls the level of cysteine sulfenylation, and provides
CC       reducing equivalents to rescue oxidatively damaged secreted
CC       proteins. Acts by transferring its disulfide bond to other
CC       proteins and is reduced in the process. DsbC is reoxidized by
CC       DsbD. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19965429}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8168498}.
