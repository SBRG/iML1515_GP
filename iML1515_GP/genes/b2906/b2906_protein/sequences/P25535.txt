CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:23709220}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells lacking this gene have a low level of
CC       coenzyme Q8 in aerobic conditions, and accumulate a compound
CC       derived from the Q biosynthetic pathway which was identified as 3-
CC       octaprenyl-4-hydroxyphenol. When grown anaerobically, they have a
CC       content of Q8 comparable with that in wild-type cells and do not
CC       accumulate 3-octaprenyl-4-hydroxyphenol. The levels of the
CC       isoprenoid naphthoquinones, demethylmenaquinone and menaquinone
CC       (MK8) are not affected in the deletion mutant under aerobic
CC       conditions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23709220}.
