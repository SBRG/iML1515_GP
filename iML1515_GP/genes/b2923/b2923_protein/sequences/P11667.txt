CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the export of arginine. Important to control
CC       the intracellular level of arginine and the correct balance
CC       between arginine and lysine. May also be involved in the export of
CC       canavanine (a plant-derived antimetabolite).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15150242}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Transcriptionally regulated by ArgP in response to the
CC       accumulation of intracellular arginine or canavanine. Lysine has a
CC       negative effect on the expression of argO.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the LysE/ArgO transporter (TC 2.A.75)
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
