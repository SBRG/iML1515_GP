CC   -!- FUNCTION: Pyrophosphatase that hydrolyzes the non-canonical purine
CC       nucleotides deoxyinosine triphosphate (dITP) and xanthosine
CC       triphosphate (XTP) as well as 2'-deoxy-N-6-hydroxylaminopurine
CC       triposphate (dHAPTP) to their respective monophosphate
CC       derivatives. Probably excludes non-canonical purines from DNA
CC       precursor pool, thus preventing their incorporation into DNA and
CC       avoiding chromosomal lesions. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_01405,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17090528}.
