CC   -!- FUNCTION: Exhibits a robust glutathione (GSH)-dependent disulfide-
CC       bond reductase activity toward the model substrate, 2-hydroxyethyl
CC       disulfide; the actual physiological substrates are not known. Also
CC       displays a modest GSH-dependent peroxidase activity toward several
CC       organic hydroperoxides, such as cumene hydroperoxide and linoleic
CC       acid 13(S)-hydroperoxide, but does not reduce H(2)O(2) or tert-
CC       butyl hydroperoxide at appreciable rates. Exhibits little or no
CC       GSH transferase activity with most typical electrophilic
CC       substrates, and has no detectable transferase activity toward 1-
CC       chloro-2,4-dinitrobenzene (CDNB) with glutathionylspermidine
CC       (GspSH) as the nucleophilic substrate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21222452}.
