CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the stereospecific, NADPH-dependent reduction
CC       of L-glyceraldehyde 3-phosphate (L-GAP). The physiological role of
CC       gpr is the detoxification of L-GAP, which may be formed by non-
CC       enzymatic racemization of GAP. Also involved in the stress
CC       response as a methylglyoxal reductase which converts the toxic
CC       metabolite methylglyoxal to acetol in vitro and in vivo.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12583903, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16077126,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18620424}.

CC         {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16077126};
