CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the aminotransferase reaction from putrescine
CC       to 2-oxoglutarate, leading to glutamate and 4-aminobutanal, which
CC       spontaneously cyclizes to form 1-pyrroline. Is also able to
CC       transaminate cadaverine and, in lower extent, spermidine, but not
CC       ornithine. Alpha-ketobutyrate and pyruvate can also act as amino
CC       acceptors, although much less efficiently.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12617754}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Putrescine + 2-oxoglutarate = L-glutamate + 1-
CC       pyrroline + H(2)O.

CC         Evidence={ECO:0000305};

CC         Note=The kinetic constants are determined for the recombinant
CC         His(6)-tagged protein and seem to be 3-4 times lower for the
