CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes, although with low efficiency, the sulfur
CC       transfer reaction from thiosulfate to cyanide. The relatively low
CC       affinity of GlpE for both thiosulfate and cyanide suggests that
CC       these compounds are not the physiological substrates. Thioredoxin
CC       1 or related dithiol proteins could instead be the physiological
CC       sulfur-acceptor substrate. Possible association with the
CC       metabolism of glycerol-phosphate remains to be elucidated.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Thiosulfate + cyanide = sulfite + thiocyanate.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer.
