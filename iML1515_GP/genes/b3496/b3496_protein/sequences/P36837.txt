CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proton-dependent permease that transports di- and
CC       tripeptides. Has a clear preference for dipeptides and tripeptides
CC       composed of L-amino acids, and discriminates dipeptides on the
CC       basis of the position of charges within the substrate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18485005}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Inhibited by CCCP but unaffected by sodium
CC       depletion. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18485005}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Although DtpB appears to represent a system of
CC       lower affinity, there is an obvious redundancy in substrate
CC       specificity as compared with DtpA.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the PTR2/POT transporter (TC 2.A.17)
CC       family. DtpB subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
