CC       moiety to the outer 3-deoxy-D-manno-octulosonic acid (Kdo) residue
CC       of a Kdo(2)-lipid A. Phosphatidylethanolamines with one
CC       unsaturated acyl group functions as pEtN donors and the reaction
CC       releases diacylglycerol. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15795227}.

CC       (2->6)-lipid A. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15795227}.
