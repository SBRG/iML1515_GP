CC   -!- FUNCTION: This enzyme may serve as a scavenger, allowing the cell
CC       to utilize biotin sulfoxide as a biotin source. It reduces a
CC       spontaneous oxidation product of biotin, D-biotin D-sulfoxide (BSO
CC       or BDS), back to biotin. Also exhibits methionine-(S)-sulfoxide
CC       (Met-S-SO) reductase activity, acting specifically on the (S)
CC       enantiomer in the free, but not the protein-bound form. It thus
CC       plays a role in assimilation of oxidized methionines.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15601707, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2180922}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: L-methionine + thioredoxin disulfide + H(2)O =
CC       L-methionine (S)-S-oxide + thioredoxin.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15601707}.
