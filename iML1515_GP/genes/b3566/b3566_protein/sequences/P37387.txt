CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the high-affinity D-xylose membrane
CC       transport system. Binds with high affinity to xylose.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: E.coli has two D-xylose transport systems that
CC       accumulate sugar against a concentration gradient: the XylE system
CC       which utilizes the electrochemical gradient of protons and that is
CC       insensitive to cold osmotic shock and the XylF system that uses a
CC       high-energy phosphate compound and is sensitive to cold osmotic
CC       shock.
