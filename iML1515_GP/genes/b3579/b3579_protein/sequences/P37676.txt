CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the tripartite ATP-independent periplasmic
CC       (TRAP) transport system YiaMNO involved in the uptake of 2,3-
CC       diketo-L-gulonate. This protein specifically binds 2,3-diketo-L-
CC       gulonate. Is not able to bind either L-ascorbate or
CC       dehydroascorbate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex comprises the extracytoplasmic solute
CC       receptor protein YiaO, and the two transmembrane proteins YiaM and
CC       YiaN.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129}.
CC   -!- MASS SPECTROMETRY: Mass=33468.8; Mass_error=2;
CC       Method=Electrospray; Range=25-328;
CC       Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129};
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the bacterial solute-binding protein 7
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- CAUTION: Was originally proposed to be a subunit from an L-
CC       xylulose uptake system, but PubMed:16385129 shows that YiaO does
CC       not bind L- or D-xylulose. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:14668138}.
