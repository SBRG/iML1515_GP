CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes two steps in the biosynthesis of coenzyme A.
CC       In the first step cysteine is conjugated to 4'-phosphopantothenate
CC       to form 4-phosphopantothenoylcysteine (PubMed:11278255). In the
CC       second the latter compound is decarboxylated to form 4'-
CC       phosphopantotheine (PubMed:10922366).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10922366, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11278255}.
