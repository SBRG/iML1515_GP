CC   -!- FUNCTION: Low-affinity potassium transport system. Interacts with
CC       Trk system potassium uptake protein TrkA. Requires TrkE (sapD) for
CC       transport activity, 20% more uptake is seen with both SapD and
CC       SapF (PubMed:11700350). Transport in the absence of SapD and SapF
CC       is dependent on a high membrane potential and a high cytoplasmic
CC       ATP concentration, suggesting this protein may be able to interact
CC       with other ATP-binding proteins (PubMed:11700350). Can transport
CC       potassium and rubidium (PubMed:7896723). {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11700350, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7896723}.
