CC   -!- FUNCTION: GTP-binding protein that is not required for the
CC       biosynthesis of Mo-molybdopterin guanine dinucleotide (Mo-MGD)
CC       cofactor, and not necessary for the formation of active
CC       molybdoenzymes using this form of molybdenum cofactor. May act as
CC       an adapter protein to achieve the efficient biosynthesis and
CC       utilization of MGD. Displays a weak intrinsic GTPase activity. Is
CC       also able to bind the nucleotides ATP, TTP and GDP, but with lower
CC       affinity than GTP. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12682065,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9219527}.
