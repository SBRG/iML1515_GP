CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for disulfide bond formation in some
CC       periplasmic proteins such as PhoA or OmpA. Acts by transferring
CC       its disulfide bond to other proteins and is reduced in the
CC       process. DsbA is reoxidized by DsbB. Required for pilus
CC       biogenesis. PhoP-regulated transcription is redox-sensitive, being
CC       activated when the periplasm becomes more reducing (deletion of
CC       dsbA/dsbB, treatment with dithiothreitol). MgrB acts between
CC       DsbA/DsbB and PhoP/PhoQ in this pathway.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1429594, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22267510}.
