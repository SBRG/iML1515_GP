CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the heme biosynthesis. Catalyzes the
CC       anaerobic oxidative decarboxylation of propionate groups of rings
CC       A and B of coproporphyrinogen III to yield the vinyl groups in
CC       protoporphyrinogen IX. It can use NAD or NADP, but NAD is
CC       preferred. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12114526,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7768836}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Coproporphyrinogen III + 2 S-adenosyl-L-
CC       methionine = protoporphyrinogen IX + 2 CO(2) + 2 L-methionine + 2
CC       5'-deoxyadenosine. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12114526}.
