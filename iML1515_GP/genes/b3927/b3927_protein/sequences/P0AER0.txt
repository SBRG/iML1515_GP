CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transporter of glycerol across the cytoplasmic membrane,
CC       with limited permeability to water and small uncharged compounds
CC       such as polyols.
CC   -!- COFACTOR:
CC       Name=Mg(2+); Xref=ChEBI:CHEBI:18420;
CC       Note=Binds 2 magnesium ions per subunit.;
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Aquaporins contain two tandem repeats each containing
CC       three membrane-spanning domains and a pore-forming loop with the
CC       signature motif Asn-Pro-Ala (NPA).
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: The remarkable property of effective water
CC       conductance combined with a strict exclusion of all ions including
CC       protons is mediated by two conserved asparagines which force a
CC       central water molecule to serve strictly as a hydrogen bond donor
CC       to its neighboring water molecules. Assisted by the electrostatic
CC       potential generated by two half-membrane spanning loops, this
CC       dictates opposite orientations of water molecules in the two
CC       halves of the channel, and thus prevents the formation of a
CC       "proton wire", while permitting rapid water diffusion.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the MIP/aquaporin (TC 1.A.8) family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
