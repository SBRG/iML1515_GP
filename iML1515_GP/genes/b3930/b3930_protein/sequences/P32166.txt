CC   -!- FUNCTION: Conversion of 1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoate (DHNA) to
CC       demethylmenaquinone (DMK). Attaches octaprenylpyrophosphate, a
CC       membrane-bound 40-carbon side chain to DHNA. The conversion of
CC       DHNA to DMK proceeds in three stages: the removal of the carboxyl
CC       group of DHNA as CO(2), the attachment of the isoprenoid side
CC       chain, and a quinol-to-quinone oxidation, which is thought to be
CC       spontaneous. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9573170}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: An all-trans-polyprenyl diphosphate + 1,4-
CC       dihydroxy-2-naphthoate = a demethylmenaquinol + diphosphate +
CC       CO(2). {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_01937,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9573170}.
