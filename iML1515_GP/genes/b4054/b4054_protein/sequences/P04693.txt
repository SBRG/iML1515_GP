CC   -!- FUNCTION: Broad-specificity enzyme that catalyzes the

CC       phenylpyruvate to yield leucine, tyrosine, and phenylalanine,

CC       antibiotic mannopeptimycin produced by Streptomyces hygroscopicus
CC       NRRL3085. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19731276}.
