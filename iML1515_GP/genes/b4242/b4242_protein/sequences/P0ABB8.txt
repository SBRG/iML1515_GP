CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15919996}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Induced by low levels of proline and by osmotic shock.
CC       The leader of mgtA mRNA functions as a riboswitch, favoring
CC       transcription under low Mg(2+) conditions. Under limiting proline
CC       levels the MgtL peptide encoded within the mgtA leader cannot be
CC       translated, thereby favoring the transcription of the mgtA ORF.
CC       Induction by osmotic shock also depends on translational
CC       regulation by MgtL (Probable). Induced by low extracellular levels
CC       of Mg(2+) via the PhoQ/PhoP two-component regulatory system.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12813061, ECO:0000305}.
