CC   -!- FUNCTION: Murein-degrading enzyme. Catalyzes the cleavage of the
CC       glycosidic bonds between N-acetylmuramic acid and N-
CC       acetylglucosamine residues in peptidoglycan. May play a role in
CC       recycling of muropeptides during cell elongation and/or cell
CC       division.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Exolytic cleavage of the (1->4)-beta-
CC       glycosidic linkage between N-acetylmuramic acid (MurNAc) and N-
CC       acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) residues in peptidoglycan, from either
CC       the reducing or the non-reducing ends of the peptidoglycan chains,
CC       with concomitant formation of a 1,6-anhydrobond in the MurNAc
CC       residue.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm. Note=Tightly associated with the
CC       murein sacculus.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the transglycosylase Slt family.
